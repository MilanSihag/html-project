About Us
OUR STORY
In 2022 we opened our first club and we are growing. At Fitness Just we believe everyone should have the opportunity to enjoy a fit and healthy lifestyle. So we have made it simple, affordable & convenient for everyone to achieve their personal health goals. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, young or old, we're here for everybody. We've seen a lot of change in the fitness industry over the years, but our philosophy has remained the same: to offer the best equipment, range of classes, knowledgeable staff and professional advice in a welcoming environment, that helps keep our members motivated.
